About Us

At Sounscribe, we are passionate about the powerful language of music. Established in 2022, we are a team of three musicians who have been close friends for over a decade. Each of us brought our unique musical talents to the table, having worked individually with prominent artists in South Africa. Our journeys were separate, but they converged into a shared vision.

About SoundScribe

our background

With backgrounds in various musical genres and experiences working with renowned artists, we realized that our collective strength lay in orchestration. It was this realization that sowed the seeds for Sounscribe. Drawing upon our individual strengths, we decided to explore the world of film, advertisements, and visual content creation. Since our inception, we have harmonized our talents, infusing our compositions with the rich tapestry of experiences gained from years in the music industry. Our friendship, mutual respect, and shared love for storytelling through music are the cornerstones of Sounscribe.

the journey
