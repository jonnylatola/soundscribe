    Did You Know?

    Here are valuable tips and answers to frequently asked questions for both managers and employees. If you have any burning concerns or lingering questions, feel free to ask us now through our chat widget and we'll help you find the answers you need.

    Can an employee withdraw/retract their resignation?

    It is the employers discretion to approve the resignation retraction and may decline the decision to withdraw the resignation.

    Are employees allowed to record the Disciplinary Hearing?

    This is allowed given that the employee has notified the employer at the beginning of the proceedings.

    When must the employer submit the EE plan to the DOL?

    A yearly report is documented and submitted to the DOL on request from the DOL or during a review period.
